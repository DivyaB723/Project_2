
### Online Food Delivery Analysis

A data driven business insight analysis that transforms raw transactional logs into a strategic roadmap for improving efficiency and driving revenue growth.

---------------------

### Project overview

This project focuses on analyzing a large-scale dataset of 100,000 records from an online food delivery platform. It examines the end-to-end lifecycle of a food order. With the help of data engineering, statistical analysis and interactive visualization, the study aims to find patterns in consumer behavior and operational performance.

--------------------

### Key Objectives

📌 Operational Efficiency:
Analyze delivery time and identify bottlenecks in the fulfillment of the orders.

📌 Revenue & Profitability:
Evaluate the benefits of promotional discounts and determine the effects of marketing campaigns on the profit.

📌 Customer Retention:
Segment users based on ordering frequency and cancellation rates.

📌 Market Trends:
Identify peak demand periods to optimize delivery partner allocation. Identify growth opportunities in new markets.

--------------------

### Methods

•	Data Cleaning

Handling of missing values using mean, median, or mode.
Removing or capping outliers.
Correcting invalid values.
Standardizing categorical values.
Ensuring logical consistency.

•	Feature  Engineering

New features like Order day type, Peak hour indicator, Profit margin percentage, Customer age groups were added for a through analysis of the dataset.

•	EDA analysis is performed to uncover hidden patterns and trends which are useful for providing business insights.

•	An interactive Power BI dashboard is created for easier understanding of market trends and performance metrics.

---------------------

### Conclusion

This analysis confirms that while the platform has a strong stable user base with a consistent Average Order Value, operational bottlenecks especially delivery delays pose a major risk to customer retention. Finding out the root cause of delivery delays and optimizing the process should be prioritized. By focusing on high-performing cuisines and stabilizing the fulfillment process in high-risk restaurants the platform can expect increase in net profit by reducing lost-revenue from cancellations.
